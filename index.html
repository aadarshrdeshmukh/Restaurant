Our Story

A journey of authentic flavors

Founded in 2010, Spice Garden brings the authentic flavors of India to Mumbai. Our chefs, trained in various regions of India, create dishes that capture the essence of traditional Indian cuisine while adding contemporary touches.

We take pride in using the finest spices and ingredients, sourced directly from different regions of India, to create an unforgettable dining experience.
